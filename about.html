Hey y'all! I'm Carolyn.

Fueled by green tea, lover of podcasts, prolific doodler

I have over a decade of experience in content marketing, including content creation, optimization, and management; social media management and advertising; SEO; graphic design; and art direction. As an Old Millennial™ I am bilingual in the languages of "What Even Is Digital Presence Management?" and "I Exited the Womb with 10k TikTok Followers." I love using my powers for good by helping small, locally-owned businesses put their best foot forward online.

I graduated from the Savannah College of Art and Design with a B.F.A. in Illustration, but I've been honing those skills since I was old enough to grasp a crayon in my chubby little hand. I work digitally and traditionally to create fine art, illustrations, and product designs that explore my obsession with plants, animals, pop culture, vintage memes, and all things creepy-cute.
